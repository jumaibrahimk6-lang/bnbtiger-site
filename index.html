Project Overview

About BNBTiger

BNBTiger is a community-focused token built on BNB Smart Chain (BSC). No presale, no private sale, no reserved team allocation — the entire supply was released at deployment.

Token basics

Fixed supply. No mint function. No burn function. No reflections, no auto-LP, no transfer taxes. A transparent, beginner-friendly BEP-20 model.

Safety & transparency

The project emphasizes clarity, simple tokenomics, published team profiles, an open-source website, and verifiable on-chain activity.
