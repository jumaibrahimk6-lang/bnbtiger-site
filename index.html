Project overview

About BNBTiger

BNB Tiger Inu (BNBTiger) is a simple, community-focused token built on BNB Smart Chain. The project emphasizes straightforward token behaviour without complex mechanics so that holders can easily understand what they own and how it works.

Token basics

BNBTiger runs as a BEP-20 contract on BSC with a fixed total supply and standard transfer behaviour. There are no advanced tokenomics such as reflections, rebasing, or automatic burning. Supply and balances can be verified entirely on-chain.

  • Network: BNB Smart Chain (BSC)
  • Symbol: BNBTiger
  • Decimals: 3

Safety & transparency

The project does not include a presale, private sale, or dedicated team allocation. There is no mint function, and the supply is fixed at deployment. Listings, integrations, and site updates are handled openly and can be tracked via the official website and explorer links.
