About BNBTiger

BNBTiger is a community-focused token built on BNB Smart Chain. The project emphasizes a straightforward token model without presale, private sale, or team-only mint functions.

The goal is to act as an easy-to-understand base asset that the community can hold, use for experiments, and integrate into tools or dashboards.

Token basics

BNBTiger runs as a BEP-20 contract on BSC with a fixed total supply and standard transfer behaviour. There are no reflections, rebasing, or automatic liquidity functions built into the contract.

All supply was made available on-chain via the deployment address, with no special rules for specific wallets beyond what is visible in the contract code.

Safety & transparency

The project does not include a presale, private sale, or dedicated team allocation. There is no mint function, and the supply is fixed at deployment.

Listings, integrations, and analytics are handled via public channels only. Users are encouraged to verify the contract on BscScan and use official links from bnbtigertoken.org.

For a more detailed description of the token, initial liquidity and project assumptions, see the BNBTiger Whitepaper (PDF) and the Team page.
