About BNBTiger

BNB Tiger Inu (BNBTiger) is a simple, community-focused token built on BNB Smart Chain. The project emphasizes straightforward token behaviour without presales, private sales, or complex vesting structures.

The goal is to act as an easy-to-understand base asset that the community can hold, use for experiments, and integrate into tools or dashboards.

Token basics

BNBTiger runs as a BEP-20 contract on BSC with a fixed total supply and standard transfer behaviour. There are no reflections, rebasing, or automatic liquidity functions built into the contract.

All supply was made available on-chain via the deployment address, with no special rules for specific wallets beyond what is visible in the contract code.

Safety & transparency

The project does not include a presale, private sale, or dedicated team allocation. There is no mint function, and the supply is fixed at deployment. Listings, integrations, and analytics are handled via public channels only.

Users are encouraged to independently verify the contract on BscScan and rely only on links shared on this official website or on the official social channels.
